Giulia is a UX/UI Designer based in Italy. Currently shipping consumer products at Bending Spoons.

Currently focused on Remini and Komoot, where I work on growth experiments, design systems, and end-to-end product flows — from research to ship.

I care about clarity, speed of iteration, and writing copy that doesn't insult the reader. I like working close to engineering, where design decisions meet real constraints, and I tend to choose the boring solution that ships over the clever one that doesn't.

Outside of work I travel, write, and occasionally run. Available for select consulting work.
