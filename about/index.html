I'm a Product Designer based in Italy. Currently shipping consumer products at Bending Spoons.

I work on consumer products at scale, end to end. Right now that means Remini and Komoot, where I take features and experiments from research to ship — competitor analysis, UX, UI, design system components, working with engineering all the way to QA. Some days I'm sketching the next paywall, some days I'm in BigQuery checking whether last week's idea moved a number.

I spend a lot of time on the part nobody puts in a portfolio: making design and engineering work better together. Shared libraries, tighter handoffs, design tokens that don't drift, faster ways to ship a variant. The closer those loops get, the better the product gets.

I like working with new tools — Cursor, AI-assisted flows, anything that shortens the loop between idea and ship. I read about behavioral design and decision-making because the small choices we make as designers are usually the ones that decide how a product feels.

The work I'm proudest of is the one users don't notice — flows that feel obvious in retrospect, copy that gets out of the way, decisions that hold up after a year of edge cases.

Outside of work I travel, go to music festivals, work out, and be with the people I love.
